Directions

  • From Lambert Airport: Take I-70 East, Merge onto I-70S via exit 238B, Merge onto I-64 East/US-40 East, Exit at Kingshighway Blvd Exit 36A, turn slight left onto Kingshighway, turn first right onto Barnes-Jewish Hospital Plaza. Turn Right onto Euclid Ave. Division is on the left at 706 S Euclid Ave just past McKinley Street. Parking is available at meters or in University Lot with a permit. From Illinois or the East: Come across the Poplar Street Bridge and keep in the left lane to pick up I-64/US-40. Exit at Kingshighway Blvd Exit 36A. Turn right onto Barnes-Jewish Hospital Plaza. Turn Right onto Euclid Ave. Division is on the left at 706 S Euclid Ave. Parking is available at meters or in University Lot with a permit. From US-40/64 or I-44: Take the Kingshighway exit and travel north to make a right on Barnes-Jewish Hospital Plaza. Turn Right onto Euclid Ave. Division is on the left at 706 S Euclid Ave. Parking is available at meters or in University Lot with a permit.
  • From the Metrolink: Take the Metrolink to the Central West End Station and exit the station to the West. Travel up the stairs or elevator and turn left on Euclid Ave. Division is on the left at 706 S Euclid Ave, just south of the intersection of McKinley and Euclid Ave.
  • From the Parkway Hotel: Exit hotel and turn left, Turn left on Euclid Ave and proceed South a few blocks to 706 S Euclid Ave, just south of the intersection of McKinley and Euclid Ave.
  • Security Escort: While on campus, please feel welcome to contact Protective Services at 2-HELP (314-362-4357) for an escort.
