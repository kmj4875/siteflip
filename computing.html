    Services and Charges

    WUBIOS Computing Facility Phil Miller, Director phil@wubios.wustl.edu (314) 362-3617 The mission of the WUBIOS Computing Facility (WUBIOS) is to provide support for storage and analysis of data. In this capacity the facility supports both the Division and its primary collaborators. Services and Charges Services provided by the WUBIOS Computing Facility include access to the facility to store, manage and analyze data. The cost is $5,100/FTE (7/1/11) per Biostatistics faculty and staff charged to the project. These charges are billed on a monthly basis through the FIS system in the University. For current rates, please contact Jocelyn Johnson, jocelyn@wubios.wustl.edu. July 1, 2016 our WUBIOS Computing Facility rate will increase from $5,100 per FTE to $5,600 per FTE.

    WUBIOS Computing Facility Staff

    The Director of the WUBIOS Computing Facility is Professor J. Philip Miller, phil@wubios.wustl.edu. His staff includes one full-time Systems Administrator, Erich Schraer, erich@wubios.wustl.edu, and one support staff, Rita Fulton, rita@wubios.wustl.edu.
